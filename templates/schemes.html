Face Bank Financial Schemes

Face Bank offers a variety of financial schemes designed to support different types of customers such as students, senior citizens, professionals, and long-term investors. Our schemes are built to help customers grow their savings, earn better returns, and enjoy secure digital banking services.

1. Senior Citizen Savings Scheme

This scheme is specially designed for individuals aged 60 years and above. It provides higher interest rates compared to normal savings accounts and ensures safe investment with stable returns.

Key Features

  • Higher interest rate compared to regular savings accounts
  • Quarterly interest payout
  • Government supported secure investment
  • Flexible deposit options

Eligibility

  • Age must be 60 years or above
  • Valid government identity proof required
  • Minimum deposit amount required

2. Student Savings Account

The Student Savings Account is designed for students who want to start learning financial management early. It provides easy digital banking with minimal charges and basic banking facilities.

Key Features

  • Zero or very low minimum balance
  • Free debit card for students
  • Mobile banking and online banking access
  • Special cashback offers on education platforms

Benefits

  • Encourages financial discipline
  • Safe digital transactions
  • Exclusive student offers

3. Fixed Deposit Plans

Fixed Deposit (FD) plans allow customers to invest money for a fixed period and earn guaranteed interest. This is ideal for customers looking for stable and predictable returns.

Plan Options

  • Short Term FD (6 months – 1 year)
  • Medium Term FD (1 – 3 years)
  • Long Term FD (3 – 10 years)

Advantages

  • Guaranteed interest returns
  • Flexible tenure options
  • Loan facility against FD
  • Automatic renewal option

4. Digital Banking Rewards Program

Face Bank encourages customers to use secure digital banking services. Through this program, customers can earn reward points for using online banking features such as fund transfers, bill payments, and mobile banking transactions.

Reward Activities

  • Online fund transfers
  • Bill payments through internet banking
  • Mobile banking usage
  • UPI and digital payments

Rewards

  • Cashback offers
  • Reward points redeemable for vouchers
  • Special partner discounts

Why Choose Face Bank?

  • Secure digital banking system
  • Advanced facial recognition authentication
  • 24/7 online banking services
  • Fast and reliable transactions
  • Customer friendly banking schemes

For more information about these schemes, customers can contact the Face Bank support team or visit the nearest branch office.
