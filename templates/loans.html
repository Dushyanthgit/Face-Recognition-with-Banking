Loan Services

Face Bank offers a variety of loan services to support your personal, educational, housing, and business goals with flexible repayment options and competitive interest rates.

👤Personal Loan Quick Approval

Personal loans are designed to help customers manage unexpected financial needs such as medical expenses, travel costs, home renovations, or other personal requirements. No collateral required.

📊 Starting from 10.5% p.a.

✨ Key Features

  • Quick approval and fast disbursement within 24-48 hours
  • No collateral or security required
  • Flexible repayment tenure from 1 to 5 years
  • Simple documentation process with minimal paperwork

📋 Eligibility

  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Stable source of income with proof
  • Valid identification and active bank account

🎓Education Loan

Education loans are provided to support students who wish to pursue higher education in universities or colleges. Covers tuition fees, accommodation, books, and other academic costs.

📊 Starting from 8.5% p.a.

✨ Key Features

  • Covers tuition fees and all study-related expenses
  • Flexible repayment after course completion (moratorium period)
  • Affordable interest rates specially for students
  • Support for both domestic and international education

🎁 Benefits

  • Financial support for higher education dreams
  • Lower interest rates compared to personal loans
  • Long repayment tenure up to 15 years

🏠Home Loan

Home loans help customers purchase, build, or renovate a house. Face Bank offers competitive interest rates and long repayment periods to make home ownership easier and more affordable.

📊 Starting from 8.25% p.a.

✨ Key Features

  • High loan amount availability up to 90% of property value
  • Long repayment tenure up to 30 years
  • Competitive interest rates with special offers
  • Option for early repayment without penalties

✅ Advantages

  • Affordable monthly installments (EMI)
  • Flexible repayment structure with step-up options
  • Support for buying, building, or renovating homes
